John E. Price

Chief Financial Officer
& Managing Director

Mr. Price is the Chief Financial Officer and a Managing Director of Salient Partners, L.P. He also serves as the Principal Financial Officer and Treasurer on the firm's investment funds.

Prior to joining the firm in 2003, Mr. Price assisted in the establishment of a family office and private investment holding company to manage the affairs and investments for a high net worth Texas family. Mr. Price was involved in all facets of management and operations including investments, accounting, finance and risk management.

Mr. Price was previously employed with Arthur Andersen, LLP, where he was a Manager in the Assurance and Business Advisory Services department. He started his career as an analyst and then later became a broker for a commercial insurance wholesaler.

He earned his undergraduate degree in Accounting, summa cum laude, from Texas Christian University. He obtained his Masters of Business Administration, graduating with honors, from the Executive MBA Program at Rice University.

Mr. Price is a licensed certified public accountant, and is a member of the Texas Society for Certified Public Accountants and the American Institute for Certified Public Accountants.
