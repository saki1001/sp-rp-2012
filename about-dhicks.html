David E. Hicks, CFA

Director

David E. Hicks is a Director of Investments at Salient Partners, where he participates in the development and marketing of alternative investment programs including socially responsible investing.

Before joining the firm, Mr. Hicks worked with AIM Investments as a Research Analyst and a Sr. Internal Wholesaler. As an analyst, he completed performance and risk analysis on funds and financial markets. Prior to that, he worked with Morgan Stanley as a Financial Advisor.

Mr. Hicks earned a Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance from Texas A&M University and a Master of Business Administration in Finance from the University of St. Thomas, Cameron School of Business. He holds the Series 7 and Series 66 licenses.

Mr. Hicks is a member of the CFA Institute and the CFA Society of Houston. He is a CFA charterholder.
