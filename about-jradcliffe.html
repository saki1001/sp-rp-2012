Jeremy L. Radcliffe

Chief Operating Officer
& Managing Director

Jeremy L. Radcliffe is a Co-Founder and Managing Director of Salient, where he oversees the firm's investment division. He also serves as the Portfolio Manager of the Salient Absolute Return Fund. Previously, Mr. Radcliffe was a Partner of The Redstone Companies, L.P., where he managed certain private equity investments.

A native of Houston, he graduated from St. John's School before earning his undergraduate degree in Classics, magna cum laude, from Princeton University. Mr. Radcliffe is a Board Member and Treasurer of the Houston READ Commission, the city's non-profit urban literacy coalition. He also assists KIPP Academy, a nationally-recognized charter school for underserved students with a large presence in Houston, in its fundraising efforts as a member of its KIPP Houston Development Committee.
