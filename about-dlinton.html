David Linton

Head of Sales/Intermediary
Services & Managing Director

David Linton is a Managing Director at Salient, where he also serves as Head of Retail Sales and Intermediary Services. In this role, he is responsible for internal/external sales, national accounts and is a member of the senior management team. Mr. Linton has more than 25 years of experience in retail sales and distribution.

Prior to joining Salient, Mr. Linton served as the Head of Sales for Van Kampen Investments, a division of Morgan Stanley, where he ran internal/external sales, national accounts closed-end funds and Unit Investment Trusts. Mr. Linton led a team of more than 150 professionals in the distribution of mutual funds, closed-end funds, separate accounts, UITs, variable annuities and 401(K)s. He was also a Managing Director for Morgan Stanley Investment Management. Prior to that, Mr. Linton held National Sales Manager roles for Sun America Mutual Funds, ING Pilgrim and Northstar Funds.

Mr. Linton earned his BA in Economics from Saint Lawrence University and currently resides in Hingham, MA with his wife Lisa and their three kids.
