Adam L. Thomas

Director & Partner

Adam L. Thomas is a Director and Partner of Salient, where he serves as Portfolio Manager. In this capacity, Mr. Thomas oversees the day to day management of each portfolio in accordance with the Funds' stated strategic investment and risk management objectives.

Previously, Mr. Thomas served as an associate at The Redstone Companies, a Houston-based investment firm, where he researched, monitored, and reported on certain alternative investment relationships.

Mr. Thomas began his career as a financial analyst with Albrecht & Associates Inc., a boutique investment banking firm that brokers asset sales of petroleum-producing properties.

Mr. Thomas earned a Masters of Business Administration from the University of Texas at Austin and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Colgate University.
