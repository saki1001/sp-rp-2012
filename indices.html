An Industry First Tool

in Alternative Investing

The Salient Risk Parity Index ("SRPI") is a composite, USD based, total return index that seeks to enable investment managers to measure the performance and effectiveness of their risk-parity strategies against an industry standard, passive index.

This first-of-its-kind Index is designed to represent the performance of an equally risk-weighted allocation to global equity, interest rate, credit and commodity exposures. One quarter of the Index's variance comes from each of the four asset classes and portfolio weights are rebalanced monthly.

The Index weights aim to balance the risk contribution for each asset class to target a 10% standard deviation for the index as a whole, a risk level which is approximately equal to the long-run annual standard deviation of a typical portfolio consisting of 60% equities and 40% debt.

Historical and ongoing Index values are calculated by a third-party calculation agent, Indxis Inc.
