Heinrich Grobler, CPA

Director of Financial Reporting

Heinrich Grobler is a Director of the Adviser and a Director at Salient Partners. He is responsible for overseeing the accounting and financial reporting for the company and its investment funds. He has over 12 years of accounting and finance experience. Prior to joining the firm, Mr. Grobler was an Audit Senior Manager with PricewaterhouseCoopers, where he was part of the Asset Management group focusing on registered investment companies, private equity funds and banking.

Mr. Grobler is a licensed certified public accountant, and is a member of the America Institute for Certified Public Accountants. He is also qualified as a Chartered Accounts in South Africa. He holds a Bachelors of Accounting (Hons.) from the University of Pretoria.
