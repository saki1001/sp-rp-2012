Andrew B. Linbeck

Managing Director

Andrew B. Linbeck is a Co-Founder and Managing Director of Salient Partners, L.P. With expertise in Business Development, Mr. Linbeck provides a broad range of investment experience and service related solutions to Salient’s major relationships. Mr. Linbeck serves on the Salient Board of Directors as well as many of the Investment Committees for the firm and suite of unique fund offerings.

Before forming Salient Partners, L.P., Mr. Linbeck was a Partner and Executive Officer of The Redstone Companies, L.P., a Houston-based investment firm. Prior to that, he served as Senior-Vice President in the Private Wealth Management Group at PaineWebber, Inc. Mr. Linbeck began his career in the financial services industry with Kidder Peabody in 1987.

Mr. Linbeck earned his undergraduate degree from the University of Notre Dame and later graduated from the Executive Management Program at the Jones School of Business at Rice University.

Mr. Linbeck serves on the board of numerous organizations, including: Catholic Charities USA, Duchesne Academy of the Sacred Heart, St. Mary’s Seminary, Bridges to Life, and Pathways for Little Feet and Paradisus Dei. Mr. Linbeck is also a member of River Oaks Country Club, Castle Pines Golf Club and The Floridian.

Mr. Linbeck is a practicing Roman Catholic, has been married for more than 20 years to his wife Shanna with whom he has three daughters.
