Salient Risk Parity V12+Index

The Salient Risk Parity V12+ Index represents a quantitatively driven global asset allocation framework with the goal of outperforming the Salient Risk Parity Index over longer time horizons on a risk adjusted basis. The Index is calculated daily, rebalanced monthly, and targets a 12% volatility level.
