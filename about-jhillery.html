Julian Hillery, CFA, CPA

Associate

Julian Hillery is an Associate in the Investments Group where he is responsible for the firm's model portfolio strategies which invest across a variety of asset classes. Mr. Hillery is also the lead manager for the Salient REITs and Salient Utilities portfolios.

Mr. Hillery previously served as an equity analyst in the asset management department of Davenport & Company. During his tenure, his work encompassed valuation across a variety of equity sectors including Real Estate and Energy, as well as other income generating opportunities. He also worked as Controller for a privately-held company in the international shipping industry.

Mr. Hillery earned his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of Richmond, in Richmond, VA. He has lived in Japan and speaks conversational Japanese. Mr. Hillery is a CFA charterholder and member of the CFA Institute and the CFA Society of Houston. He is also CPA licensed in Virginia.
