Key Personnel

The Salient Risk Parity Index Committee selects, maintains, and reviews the list of futures contracts and credit default swap indices that comprise the Salient Risk Parity Index and oversees the computation of Index weights for each asset. The committee is chaired by Lee Partridge. Other members of the Index committee are: Roberto Croce, Heinrich Grobler, Jeremy Radcliffe and Adam Thomas.
